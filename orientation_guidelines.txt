AI Orientation Guidelines

The AI uses real-time guidance messages to ensure the user is standing correctly before counting their reps. Different exercises require different camera perspectives. 

---

### 1. Exercises Requiring a SIDE Profile
For these exercises, the camera needs to see the side of the user's body to accurately measure the bend of their spine, hips, knees, or elbows.

* Bicep Curls
  - AI Form Check Focus: Elbow angle & shoulder sway
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Turn sideways for tracking!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Begin exercise!"

* Bodyweight Squats
  - AI Form Check Focus: Hip crease depth & spine angle
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Turn sideways so I can see depth!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Perfect side profile. Start squatting."

* Hinging (Deadlifts/RDLs)
  - AI Form Check Focus: Lower back rounding & hip hinge
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Turn sideways to track your lower back!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Back is visible. Begin hinging."

* Planks / Core Holds
  - AI Form Check Focus: Hip drop (hips sagging or raised)
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Turn sideways to your camera!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Hold that position!"

---

### 2. Exercises Requiring a FRONT Profile
For these exercises, the camera needs to see the front of the user to track symmetry (e.g., are both arms moving together? Are the knees caving inward?).

* Pressing (Shoulder Press)
  - AI Form Check Focus: Arm extension & symmetry
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Face the camera directly!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Press up!"

* Jumping Jacks
  - AI Form Check Focus: Arm/leg spread width
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Face forward for Jumping Jacks!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Start jumping!"

* High Knees
  - AI Form Check Focus: Knee height comparison
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Face the camera directly!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Drive those knees!"

* Pulling (Rows/Pullups)
  - AI Form Check Focus: Symmetrical shoulder movement
  - Dynamic Guidance Message (Red): ⚠️ "Face the camera directly!"
  - Success Message (Green): ✅ "Ready. Begin pulling."
